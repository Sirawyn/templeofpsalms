ARE WE DOOMED TO BE FOREVER RULED BY THE CRUEL, THE VIOLENT, AND THE BRUTAL? IF THERE WAS SUCH A WAY TO EXCISE SUCH A WORLD, IS IT NOT THE PURPOSE OF THOSE WITH WILL AND STRENGTH TO DO SO? AND FOR THOSE WITHOUT SUCH QUALITIES, IS IT NOT THEIR OBLIGATION TO FIND IT WITHIN THEMSELVES?

— PRAYER OF SWORDS —
